FAQ
What does Vibe Day do?
In a new city, Vibe Day creates the best plan for your vibe, time, and budget—either as a ready route or smart suggestions you can pick from.
When is it most useful?
Especially when you’re traveling as a tourist and don’t know what to do in a new city.
What’s the difference between Planner and Route?
Planner suggests great places—you choose and save. Route generates a personalized route based on your time window, vibe, and budget.
How long does it take to create a plan?
Seconds.
What do I choose to build a route?
Time window, vibe, who you’re with, budget, transport mode, and optional hashtags.
Can I edit the route?
Yes. Save your route and remove stops you don’t like.
Are recommendations up to date?
Yes. We keep recommendations fresh based on real user experiences.
Why do you need my location?
Location helps us give you accurate, relevant suggestions near you.
Does it work worldwide?
Yes—Vibe Day is designed to be used in countries and cities worldwide.
Is there offline mode?
Not yet—Vibe Day requires an internet connection.
